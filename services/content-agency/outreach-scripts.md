## 📈 Success Metrics to Track

For each client, track:
1. **Follower Growth** (target: +20% per month)
2. **Engagement Rate** (likes, comments, shares)
3. **Website Traffic** (if they have a site)
4. **Leads Generated** (via WhatsApp/phone)
5. **Sales Conversion** (if they track it)

Use these metrics to show value and upsell!
